Installation Complete! Thank you for installing LightBar For Keyboard.

Quick Tips:
1. To access settings, just RIGHT-CLICK anywhere on the white light bar.
2. From the right-click menu, you can easily switch between "Docked" and "Floating" modes, or exit the application completely.
3. If you chose to create a desktop shortcut, you can start the app from there anytime.
