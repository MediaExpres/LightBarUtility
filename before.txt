Welcome to the LightBar For Keyboard Setup!

This utility provides a discrete, controllable light source directly from your monitor, perfect for typing in low-light environments without straining your eyes.

Key Features:
* Docked Mode: Automatically reserves space at the bottom of your screen so windows don't overlap.
* Floating Mode: Drag the light bar anywhere on your screen.
* Unobtrusive: No taskbar icon to ensure zero distractions.
